Specialist Training in Psychiatry & Psychoanalysis

I am a psychiatry registrar (doctor in specialist training) with clinical experience in mental health care, and a member of the Freudian School of Melbourne – School of Lacanian Psychoanalysis.
My work integrates contemporary psychiatric knowledge with the depth of psychoanalytic theory, offering a space for those seeking understanding beyond symptoms—toward the truth of their desire.

About Me

My name is Tianran Wu. I provide consultations and psychoanalytic sessions for individuals who wish to explore their unconscious, address persistent difficulties, or engage in a process of subjective transformation.

My approach draws on:

• Medical expertise in psychiatry: informed by evidence-based practice and clinical training.
• Lacanian psychoanalysis: a rigorous method that privileges speech, listening, and the singularity of each subject.

Whether you are struggling with anxiety, depression, obsessive thoughts, or simply seeking a deeper understanding of yourself, psychoanalysis offers a unique path.
